Our Story & Journey

Ojoor began as a small consulting firm in Riyadh, driven by a passion for helping businesses navigate the Saudi regulatory landscape. As demand grew, our services expanded to include payroll management, recruitment, and legal & accounting advisory. Today, we are proud to serve companies of all sizes across the Kingdom and the GCC, continuing our journey as their trusted partner in success.
